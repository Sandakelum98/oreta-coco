About Oreta Coco

Your Trusted Coconut Product Manufacturer and Exporter

Oreta Coco is a distinguished coconut-based product manufacturing company based in Sri Lanka, deeply engaged in global exports. Against the backdrop of Sri Lanka's significant growth in coconut-based products over the past decade, Oreta Coco emerged, producing premium coconut-based products tailored for export markets.As a certified entity, Oreta Coco is committed to crafting top-tier coir products for agriculture, horticulture, substrates industry, and gardens.
